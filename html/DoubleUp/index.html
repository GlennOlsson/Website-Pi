DoubleUp

Take the risk or play safe?

DoubleUp! is a game where you send in-game currency to players all around the globe, and they can then choose to DoubleUp! the money, or keep the small amount. If they choose to DoubleUp!, you will get a chance to either DoubleUp! again, or keep the money. This will go on until one player decides to keep the money.

Work out strategies and plans to gains as much money as you can!
